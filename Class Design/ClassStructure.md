# Class Structure

**Overall Theme**: Core Concept (e.g., arm bars, attachments, pressure)

## 1. Standing (10 min)
- Grip fighting
- At least one method of off balancing (kizushi)

## 2. Mobility (10 - 15 min)
- 2 or 3 games which represent previous concepts (e.g., tripod sweep, butterfly chase)

## 3. Takedowns (10 - 15 min)
- 1 or 2 takedowns which work well with our kizushi

## 4. Discussion about the Concept (max 5 minutes)
- Used as a break
- Describe mechanics

## 5. Concept Applications (25 - 30 min)
- 4 or 5 games based on our primary concept.
- Create a problem which encourages the use of our concept and allow the student to attempt their own solution.
- Propose solutions to the problem and allow the student to iterate.

## 6. Review Concepts (max 5 min)
- Lead an open discussion on any problems or successes that the students had.
- Use as a break before rolling

## 7. Free Roll (15 + minutes)
- Ensure at least 3 full rounds at the end of class.

**Note**: Have a notebook available for students to leave their questions.
