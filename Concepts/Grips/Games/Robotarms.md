---
title: Robot arms
category: Grips
players: 2
duration: 1
type: Alternating
intensity: Flow
difficulty: Beginner
goals: Offensive player should maintain a c grip on defender's forearm.
purpose: Improve grip switching
focus: Defender, keep elbows attached to their hips. Attacker should keep their thumb wrapped around their opponent's forearm without using much force.
---

Starting standing, the defending player attaches their elbows to their hip and moves their hands up and down. The offensive player is to maintain a grip on their opponent's arm, switching their grip as the angle on their opponent's forearm changes
