title: Robot arms
category: Grips
players: 2
duration: 2
type: Alternating
intensity: Flow
goals: 
purpose: 
focus: Defender, keep elbows attached to their hips. Attacker should keep their thumb wrapped around their opponent's forearm without using much force.
---

Starting standing, the defending player attaches their elbows to their hip and moves their hands up and down. The attacking player is to maintain a grip on their opponent's arm, switching their grip as the angle on their oppoenent's forearm changes
