---
title: Robot arms - Flow
category: Grips
players: 2
duration: 1
type: Round Switching
intensity: Flow
difficulty: Beginner
goals: Offsensive player needs to keep thier hands in contact with the defensive player's hands with getting over extended or tripping.
purpose: Learn how to make subtle connections to your opponent
focus: Offense should always be applying light pressure towards the defensive player's midline.
---

Starting standing. Defensive player move their hands up and down and in smooth arcs. The offesive player should lighlty follow the hands of the defensive player. 

Both players should keep a strong posture and a low base.
