---
title: 4 corners
category: Pressure
players: 2
duration: 1
type: Round Switching
intensity: Adversarial
goals: Bottom player is to roll to their side
Top player needs to keep the bottom player's back on the mat
purpose: Teaches the top player how to control by using the 4 corners of the torso
focus: Top player should use their hands, elbows, hips, or knees to apply force to one of the 4 corners ot the torso. Be mindful of the direction force is being applied.
---

Starting in side control, the top player needs to keep their opponent flat on the mat.

Variations involve not allowing the knees to touch the floor or top player not using their hands.

Bottom player cannot make attachments
