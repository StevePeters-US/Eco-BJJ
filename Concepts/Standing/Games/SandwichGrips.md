---
title: Sandwich Grips
category: Standing
players: 2
duration: 1
type: Round Switching
intensity: Adversarial
goals: Attacker must make a looping connection around their opponent.
purpose: Teaches attacker to open the elbows to gain access to the body by manipulating the knuckles.
focus: Focus on keeping the gripping fingers flat and turning the  opponents elbows out away from their body
---
